About me

I fell in love with design when I was only 14 years old. Since then I can't picture myself doing other things without involving design in it.

When I was 16 I've created one of the most accessed blogs about design in Brazil called Choco La Design. After 5 years I helped to create another project that became a reference in UI Design in the country.

My life is divided into helping the design community, creating products for companies and startups across the globe.

I'm passionate about the human mind, behavior science, products and every philosophy that helps us to become a better person.

I had the pleasure to work with companies across different fields such as finances, health, home services, food and more.
